Important WingstopFreeFries Announcement

First though, some WingstopFreeFries history and the motivation behind this announcement.

It has been just under two years since the idea of WingstopFreeFries was conceived by me. It all started with me standing in line at Wingstop (a frequent event), realizing I could get free fries by filling out a survey, but also realizing how painfully slow and repetitive the process was. I started to learn all of the technologies required in order to create this website. And what came out was a quick little website to make getting free food easier. The very first users may remember how WFF looked and operated at first. Just a simple input form with a submit button, but it was slow and definitely not something you'd want to use while standing in line.

From there, WingstopFreeFries grew beyond anything I expected. After a lot of tweaking and late-night bug fixing, I managed to cut the wait time from several minutes down to just a second. Eventually, the site expanded to support Panda Express, Rubios, and Blaze Pizza. Over time, it became a go-to tool for anyone looking to score some extra food without spending extra cash. With Final stats of 1,809 total uses and $9,341.86 saved. What started as a simple personal project turned into something that genuinely helped others.
